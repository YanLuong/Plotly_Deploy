From bacteria to beef:



We are on a mission to discover which bacteria species live in your average belly button.

This field is not well studied and much remains unknown when it comes to bacterial species on the human body.

We are particularly interested in bacteria that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. We have partnered with a food start up called Improbable Beef to research candidate species.

To discover these bacterial species navigate to the dashboard or you may volunteer to discover what bacteria species are living on your belly button!
